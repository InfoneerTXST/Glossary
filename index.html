Posted on December 28th, 2022

Property Natural Language Definition
 agentIn relationship from an agent to a process where the agent participates in the process and materially causes the occurrence of the process (plays a causal role).
 dependsOnBuyer relation from a supply relationship to a buyer (the bearer), in which the relationship mediates between the buyer and some supplier and some product or service
 dependsOnProduct relation from a supply relationship to a product (the bearer), in which the relationship mediates between the product and some supplier and customer
 dependsOnSupplier relation from a supply relationship to a supplier (the bearer), in which the relationship mediates between the supplier and some customer and some product or service
 endsAt relationship from a process to a spatial region or site whevever the spatial region or site is the location in which the process ends
 hasAgent relation that holds between a process and agent when the agent participates in the process and plays a causal role
 hasParticipantAsTradeItem relation that holds between a buying process or a supplying process and a product in which the product participates in the process as the subject of trade
 holds relationship between an agent and a material entity when the agent physically keeps or carries the material entity
 initiatesRelationship relation between an event and a relational specifically dependent continuant where the event establishes (originates) the relational specifically dependent continuant as a mediator among several other entities
 isSiteOf relationship between a site or spatial region and a process when the process occurs at the site or spatial region
 isSuppliedByAtSomeTime relationship between a commercial service or product and a supplier when it the service or product is provided by a supplier to a customer
 isSuppliedToAtSomeTime relationship between a commercial service or product and a customer when the customer buys the product or service from a supplier
 occursAt relationship from a process to a spatial region or site whenvever the spatial region or site is the location in which the process unfolds
 occursOn relation which holds between some processual entity and some temporal region whenever the duration of the processual entity is contained by the temporal region
 ownedBy relationship from a material entity to a person or business organization when the material entity is held by the person or business organization as legal property
 owns relationship from a persop or business organization to an independent continuant when the agent holds the independent continuant as legal property.
 relationshipInitiatedBy relation between a relational specifically dependent continuant and an event where the event establishes (originates) the relational specifically dependent continuant as a mediator among several other entities
 startsAt relationship from a process to a spatial region or site whevever the spatial region o site is the location in which the process starts
 suppliesToAtSomeTime relationship between a supplier and a customer when the supplier sells a product of service to a customer
Class Natural Language Definition
 Airway three-dimensional spatial region that connects one specified location to another at a specified altitude, along which an aircraft may be flown
 ApprasialProcess measurement process that involves evaluating, assessing, estimating, or judging the nature, value, importance, condition, or quality of some entity
 ArrivalProcess material location change process that consists of the participating entity reaching its destination such that the travel or transport process which it is a part of is completed
 ArtifactFunction function that inheres in some material artifact in virtue of that artifact being designed to be used in processes that require that function to be realized
 BillOfLading agreement between a shipper and a carrier that prescribes some consigning process
 Cargo material entity that is transported by transport equipment
 CargoRole role held by a material entity when it is being transported or is planned to be transported by a carrier
 Carrier person or business organization who provides transportation services
 Change A process in which some independent continuant endures and 1) one or more of the dependent entities it bears increase or decrease in intensity, 2) the entity begins to bear some dependent entity or 3) the entity ceases to bear some dependent entity.
 ClassificationScheme reference specification and scheme that defines how identification keys, data attributes and barcodes must be formulated and used in business applications
 Classifier standardized classification or delineation for something, per some scheme for such delineation, within a specified context
 CodeIdentifier Identifier that consists of a string of characters as prescribed by some identification scheme
 Consignee person or business organization that receives some shipment
 ConsigneeRole role held by a person or business organization when it receives a shipment from a carrier and owns the shipment after the receipt
 ConsigningProcess business process that results in placing a material entity in the possession of some agent without changing the ownership until the material entity is sold
 ConsignorRole role held by a business organization or person when it prepares and consigns a shipment it owns
 Container piece of transport equipment that is designed to contain some material entities
 DepartureProcess material location change process that consists of the participating entity leaving its starting location such that the travel or transport process it is a part of is initiated
 DispatchProcess business process in which an consignor (shipper) sends some shipment to a consignee (receiver)
 DistributionCenter storage facility designed to store manufactured products that are to be distributed to resellers and wholesalers
 Distributor person or business organization when it purchases products from manufacturers and resells them to wholesalers
 DistributorRole role held by a person or business organization when it buys some material products from a manufacturer and resells them to a wholesaler
 Facility engineered system that consists of buildings, structures, roads, machines, and equipment desgined to provide certain capabilities
 Factory facility that is designed for manufacturing products and includes one or more production systems
 FreightForwarder person or business organization when it arranges the transportation of shipments on behalf of the shipper (consignor) or consignee
 FreightForwarderRole logistics service provider role held by an agent when it arranges the transportation of shipments on behalf of the shipper (consignor) or consignee
 FreightForwardingBusinessFunction business function that is realized in a freight forwarding service
 FreightForwardingProcess business process of planning and coordinating the transportation of material products on behalf of a shipper
 FreightForwardingService logistics service that consists of at least one freight forwarding process
 GainOfRole change process in which some entity becomes the bearer of some role.
 GeospatialLocation geospatial site that is the location of some material entity at some time or the site of some process
 GeospatialSite site at or near the surface of the earth
 GlobalLocationNumber code identifer that uniquely identifies a business organization or a geospatial location and complies with GLN coding scheme
 GroupOfPersons group of agents that has only persons as parts
 GroupOfSuppliers group of agents involved in trading and other business relationships with one another during the operation of specific supply chains
 IndustrialInventory material product, material component, raw material, or material artifact ready to be sold or used and is stored in a storage facility or on factory floor
 IndustrialInventoryRole role held by some material entity when it is stored by a bussiness organization and it is intended to be supplied to a customer or to be used or consumed in some product production process
 Industry group of organizations that are related based on their primary business activities
 IntegratedCarrierBusinessOrganization business organization that is created for the purpose of providing different types of transportation and freight forwarding services
 Inventory material entity kept or owned by an agent to meet external or internal need some agent might have
 InventoryManagementProcess business process that consists of the activities required for controlling, tracking, recording, and planning inventroies
 InventoryRole material resource role held by a number of material entities when they are kept by an agent to satisfy some need of the agent or meet some objective carried by the agent
 Load collection of material entities which share the same transportation and transfer history
 LoadRole traceable resource unit role held by a number of material entities that have been transferred or transported together
 LocationIdentificationScheme identification scheme that prescribes how unique identifiers of physical locations are formulated
 LocationRole role held by a geospatial site when it is the location of some material entity at some time or the site of some process
 LogisticUnit material entities packaged together for transport or storage
 LogisticUnitRole traceable resource unit role held by a number of material entities that are packaged together for transport or storage
 LogisticsProcess business process that consists of a combination of one or more transport, shipment preparation, storage or receiving processes
 LogisticsService commercial service that consists of at least one transport process, packaging process, freight forwarding, or storage process
 LogisticsServiceProvider service provider role that is held by a business organization or person when it provides a logistics service
 LossOfRole change process in which some entity ceases to be the bearer of some role.
 Lot quantity of material entities produced together and sharing the same production history and specifications
 LotNumber code identifier that uniquely denotes a particular quantity of material entities that have shared production or transformation histroy.
 LotRole traceable resource unit role held by material entities that have a shared production or transformation history
 Make-To-OrderProductionCapability production capability to manufacture some material artifact only after a confirmed customer order is received
 Make-To-StockProductionCapability production capability to manufacture and store some material artifact to fulfill expected incoming orders or forcasted demand
 Manufacturer person or business organization who manufacturs products for sale or provides manufacturing services to other persons or organizations
 ManufacturingSupplyChain supply chain system that is formed to manufacture one or more products
 NAICSCode classifier that denotes an organization based on its primary business functions and complies with NAICS coding specifications
 OrganizationClassificationScheme reference specification that prescribes how organization classifiers are formulated
 PackagingPlanSpecification plan specification that prescribes a packaging process
 PackagingProcess business process of providing a protective covering to a product (material products) in such a way that it protects the product during storage and movement
 PackagingService logistics service that consists of at lest one packaging process
 PackagingServiceProviderRole logisitics service provider role held by a business organization or person when it provides a packaging service
 PartNumber code identifier that uniquely identifies an artifact (material artifact) and that is created according to some part numbering system
 PieceofTransportEquipment piece of equipment that has the function of holding, protecting, and securing a number of material entites for transportation purposes
 ProductionCapability capability of an agent or a system that is realized in a product production process
 PurchaseOrder agreement between a buyer and a vendor (supplier) that that contains a set of commitments on the buyer and the vendeor
 PurchaseOrder agreement between a buyer and a vendor (supplier) that prescribes some buying process
 RecivingProcess planned process in which an agent receives some material entity from another agent
 Retailer person or business organization who buys products from wholesalers and manufacturers and resells them to end users
 RetailerRole role held by a person or business organization when it buys products (material product) from manufacturers or wholesalers and offers them for sale to customers who are the end users of the product
 Seaway three-dimensional spatial region that connects one specified location to another on a body of water along which a watercraft may travel
 ServiceConsumerRole customer role held by a person or business organization when it receives and consumes a service
 ServiceSupplyChain supply chain system that is formed to deliver some commercial services
 ShipFromLocation geospatial location at which some dispatch process occurs as prescribed by some shippig agreement (bill of lading)
 ShipFromLocationRole location role held by a geospatial site when it is the location at which some dispatch process occures as prescribed by some shipping agreement (Bill of Lading).
 ShipToLocation geospatial location in which some receiving process occurs as prescribed by some shipping agreement (bill of lading)
 ShipToLocationRole location role held by a geospatial site when it is the location at which some receiving process occures as prescribed by some shipping agreement (Bill of Lading).
 Shipment collection of material entities delivered to a receiver's location that have undergone the same dispatch and receipt processes
 ShipmentPreparationProcess business process in which some material entities are prepared to be transported together to a receiver’s location.
 ShipmentRole traceable resource unit role held by material entities when they undergo the same dispatch and receipt processes as prescribed by a bill of lading
 ShipmentRole traceable resource unit role held by material entities when they undergo the same dispatch and receipt processes
 Shipper person or business organization that prepares and sends shipments for transport
 ShippingRoute way (three-dimensional spatial region) that connects two or more supply chain nodes along which some shipment travels during a transport process
 StorageCapability capability of an agent or a system that is realized in a storage process
 StorageFacility facility that is designed to store materials or goods
 StorageProcess planned process of putting and keeping material entities in a specified location for future use
 StorageService logistics service that consists of at least one storage process
 StorageServiceProviderRole logisitics service provider role that is held by a person or business organization when it provides a storage service
 SupplierEvaluationProcess apprasial process that involves evaluating, assessing, estimating, or judging the capabilities of a person or organization that is intended to become a member of a supply chain
 SupplyChainAgent person or business organization who supplies material products or commercial services to other agents in a supply chain
 SupplyChainAgentRole role held by an agent that is a part of a supply chain system when it supplies material products or commercial services to other agents in the supply chain system
 SupplyChainNode geospatial location that is the site of some supply chain process
 SupplyChainNodeRole location role held by a geospatial site when it is the site some supply chain process
 SupplyChainObjectiveSpecification objective specification that prescribes what the outcome of a supply chain process should be
 SupplyChainPlanSpecification plan specification that has one or more supply chain objective specifications as parts and that prescribes a supply chain process
 SupplyChainProcess planned process in which a supply chain plan specification is realized by a number of supply chain agents
 SupplyChainSystem engineered system composed of agents, facilties, machines, and information systems that is designed to deliver products and services to end customers through an engineered flow of information, material, and cash
 SupplyChainTransportProcess location change process which involves movement of goods between sending trading party’s location and receiving trading party’s locations over the public logistics pathway/infrastructure
 SupplyRelationship relationship that exists between two agents when one agent supplies a product or service to another agent and that is prescribed by an agreement
 SystemCapability capability that inheres in a system to the extent of the system's capacity to realize it in some processes
 Traceability capability of a system to trace the history, application or location of an object.
 Traceability capability of a system or organization to trace the history, application or location of an object.
 TraceableResourceUnit material entity that is uniquely identified and there may be a need to retrieve information about its history, application, or location as it moves through a supply chain
 TraceableResourceUnitRole role held by a number of uniquely identified material entities when they participate in some supply chain processes or production processes while some agent observes them and collects information about them for future retrieval
 TrackingEvent event that has at least one traceable resource unit or equipment (with which TRU comes into contact with) or operator (that runs an equipment with which TRU comes into contact with at some time) as participant .
 Trailer piece of transport equipment that is unpowered and is pulled by a powered vehicle

 TransportationBusinessFunction business function that is realized in a transportation service
 TransportationService logistics service that consists of at least one transport process
 TransportationServiceProviderRole logisitics service provider role that is held by a business organization or person when it provides a transportation service

 WarehousingPlanSpecification plan specification that prescribes a warehousing process
 WarehousingProcess business process of storing products (material products) in a storage facility for future use
 Wholesaler person or business organization that buys products from distributors and resells them to retailers or other business organizations
 WholesalerRole role held by a person or business organization when it buys some product from a distributor and resells them to retailers
